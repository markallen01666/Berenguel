Self portrait

• 2016

Summer Exhibition 2016, The Royal Academy of Arts, London, UK.

One of the explorative lines I have worked on is the body. The body as a centre, as a starting point of research and connection to the inner and outer world.

A naked woman, who looks at her reflection and take a photo of herself.

A woman who holds a camera in front of her face, faceless, she becomes somehow any woman, an anonymous woman. A hunter disguised as a lure.

A moment of reflection on oneself.

A reflection in which the spectator will also be ‘caught’ and ‘framed’.
