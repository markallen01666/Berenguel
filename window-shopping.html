Window shopping

• 2000

London Art Biennal, "Window Shopping Exhibition", organza and various textiles, poem, London, UK.

Bienal de Arte de Londres, "Exposición en vitrinas", organza y otras telas, poema, Londres, Reino Unido.

Tête-à-Tête

I was protected by

the fragile

I was defended by

the vulnerable

I was held only by

the breakable

the perishable

I was supported just by

the subtle

I was sustained by

the endlessly killable

I was with

my only protective clothing

my unique vestment

my only truth

my skin

Tête-à-Tête

Sólo me separaba

la fragilísima

me defendía

la vulnerable

sólo me sostuvoy

lo quebrantable

lo perecedero

me mantuvo

lo sutil

lo a todas luces matable

con esa

mi escafandra

mi único ropaje

mi sola verdad

mi piel
